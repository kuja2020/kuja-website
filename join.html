Eligibility Rules

Who Can Join?

  • - Regular undergraduate or postgraduate student of Khulna University
  • - Interest in writing, reporting, photography, or video journalism
  • - Must be working as a Khulna University Correspondent for a registered daily newspaper, television channel, or online news portal
  • - Minimum of two months of working experience with the respective registered media house
Tiers Overview

Membership Tiers

Associate Member

Initial tier for new student recruits starting campus news reporting workshops.

General Member

Active campus reporters writing regularly for national or local newspapers.

Executive Member

Members elected to run association administrative desks (President, General Secretary, etc.).

Application Process

How to Apply

1
Express Interest

Contact the KUJA secretariat or fill the inquiry form on our contact page.

2
Document Submission

Submit the prescribed form with the required criteria documents, including media ID card/certification and transcripts of published reports.

3
Evaluation

Must clear the basic recruitment evaluation, including document verification and a formal discussion.

4
Welcome to KUJA!

Accepted members are inducted in a formal ceremony and join active reporting.
