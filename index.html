About KUJA

Khulna University Journalists’ Association (KUJA) was officially established in 2020 with the aim of creating a unified and professional platform for student journalists at Khulna University. Its formation marked a significant transition from individual journalistic activities to a coordinated and organized body representing campus-based media contributors.

Message From President

Alkama Romin

(President)

Upholding Truth, Empowering Voices
"Journalism is the search for truth and the commitment to serve the community with integrity. At Khulna University Journalists' Association (KUJA), we strive to be the bridge between information and the students, ensuring that every story is told with accuracy and every voice is heard. Our mission is not just to report events, but to foster a culture of transparency, accountability, and excellence within our beloved campus. We remain dedicated to nurturing the next generation of journalists who will lead with courage and ethics."
