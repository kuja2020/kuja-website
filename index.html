Our Story

About KUJA

Khulna University Journalists' Association (KUJA) was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate student journalists committed to bringing a strong media voice to the campus. From its earliest days, KUJA has functioned as a collaborative newsroom where members develop real-world journalism skills.

We publish investigative reports, cover campus events, and amplify student voices on issues ranging from academic policy to administrative accountability. Our members have gone on to work with leading national dailies.

KUJA is also deeply committed to professional development — from PIB journalism training certifications to internal writing workshops — ensuring our members are well-equipped for careers in modern media.
